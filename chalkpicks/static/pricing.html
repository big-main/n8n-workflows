Frequently Asked Questions

How often are picks updated? +

Picks are rebuilt from live data at 6am, 12pm, and 5pm UTC daily, plus every 2 hours automatically. Any significant line movement triggers an immediate re-score.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. Cancel any time from your billing portal with one click. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

What sports are covered? +
